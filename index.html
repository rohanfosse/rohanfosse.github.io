AFoDIB

Description
The AFoDIB is an association created in 1994. Its purpose is to promote the doctoral training in computer science of Bordeaux as well as to facilitate the insertion of the students coming from this formation (in particular via AquiDoc, of which AFoDIB is a member). It also aims to organize events to allow doctoral students and other members of the laboratory to share convivial moments.
If you want to learn more about the AFoDIB, you can go here.
Current Role Treasurer

Maths à Modeler

The aim is to offer the general public, especially schoolchildren, to discover basic computer science and mathematics through research. We therefore propose research problems, inspired by the problems we are studying, and invite them to explore these problems by adopting the researcher's approach.
